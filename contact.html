FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often before an engagement begins.

How long does a typical engagement take?
+
It depends on scope. Discovery and architecture phases typically take 2–4 weeks. Full project delivery ranges from 6 weeks for focused solutions to 6+ months for platform-scale work.
Do you work with startups or only enterprises?
+
Both. We've worked with early-stage startups needing technical co-founders, and large enterprises needing specialised deep-tech expertise. Budget and scope are assessed case by case.
Is a free initial consultation available?
+
Yes. We offer a free 30-minute technical consultation for all new inquiries. This lets us understand your problem and you to evaluate our approach — with no commitment.
What industries do you have the most experience in?
+
Finance, healthcare, logistics, energy, and deep-tech research sectors are our strongest verticals. We've also worked across education, manufacturing, and government domains.
Do you offer retainer or ongoing support contracts?
+
Yes. We have flexible retainer arrangements for ongoing technical advisory, model maintenance, or infrastructure support. Terms are tailored to each client relationship.
